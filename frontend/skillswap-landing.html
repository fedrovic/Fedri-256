How SkillSwap Works

From signup to your first skill session in under 10 minutes.

01
🎯

Build Your Profile

Sign up with Google or email. List skills you can teach and skills you want to learn. Set your availability and timezone.

02
🔍

Discover Matches

Our smart algorithm finds users with complementary skills. Browse profiles, check verified credentials, and see availability overlap.

03
🤝

Agree & Schedule

Send a swap request. Agree on session frequency, mode (live or async), and duration. The platform handles scheduling and reminders.

04
🚀

Learn & Earn

Attend sessions via built-in video call. Earn SkillCoins when you teach. Spend them when you learn — even from someone else.
